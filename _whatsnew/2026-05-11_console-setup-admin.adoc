=== Console agent 4.7.0

The 4.7.0 release supports both standard mode and restricted mode.

This release of the Console agent includes security improvements and bug fixes.

=== NetApp Console administration

This release includes the following new feature.

.Verify your Console organization
By default, Digital Advisor shows all NetApp accounts associated with your company, rather than just your Console organization. To limit what is shown in Digital Advisor to a specific account, verify the business account you want to use with your Console organization. link:https://docs.netapp.com/us-en/console-setup-admin/task-iam-organization-verify.html[Learn how to verify your Console organization^]
